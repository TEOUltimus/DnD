Fane of the Drow and More -- Part 4
September 2005


This month's featured battle map comes from Fantastic Locations: Fane of the Drow. The other three maps can be used in conjunction with the Heroes of Battle web enhancement.





Cartography by Mike Schley and Jason Engle

09/27 Intercept: Hills, woods, and a possible route to or from other interesting terrain features make this map a useful one if you have someone trying to get somewhere specific. (That area around the Y marker could be very creepy!) Download 300dpi JPG
