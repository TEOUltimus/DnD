Campaign Reaches 7th Level
Campaign Update
by Jeff Simpson


The level kick taking Xen'drik Expeditions to 7th level is now active. The starting level for new characters is 7th level with a base character-equip value of 19,000 gp. Existing characters whose level or gp values are lower than these numbers are bumped to these new campaign amounts. Full details regarding the level kick are included in the Xen'drik Expeditions Campaign Standards v.3.0 available on the campaign's welcome page.
