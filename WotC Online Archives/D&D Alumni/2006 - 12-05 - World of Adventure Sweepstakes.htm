World of Adventure Sweepstakes


We have a winner! With over seventy entries, this year’s Fan Film Fest was a blockbuster success. Now the voting’s done and the results are in—check them out for yourself at dndfanfilmfest.com.

In fact, be sure to check out the winning film, World of Adventure, extra closely for your chance to win a copy of the latest sourcebooks: Cityscape and Fiendish Codex II: Tyrants of the Nine Hells.

Here’s how:

When the folks at Dungeon Majesty put together their winning film, they used quite a bit of artwork from past editions of the game. If you can guess at least ten of the sources they used, you’ll be entered in a chance to win these books!

The Fine Print

To enter, identify at least ten (10) sources from which artwork used in the film titled “Dungeon Majesty’s ‘World of Adventure’” that won Wizards’ D&D Fan Film Contest derive. This film can be viewed at www.dndfanfilmfest.com. Submit your entry by sending either (i) an e-mail message to dndcontest@wizards.com and enter “World of Adventure” in the subject line or (ii) a 3x5 postcard to Wizards of the Coast, D&D Fan Film Festival Sweepstakes, Online Media c/o Bart Carroll, 1600 Lind Ave. S.W., Suite 400, Renton, WA 98055. For both e-mail and mail-in entries, you must include your complete name, address, e-mail address, and daytime and evening phone numbers. All entries must be received no later than 11:59 p.m. PST on December 18, 2006. Limit one entry per person or e-mail address.

The Fine, Fine Print

Official rules can be read right here.

Thanks to everyone who participated in the Fan Film Fest! And good luck to everyone participating in the World of Adventure Sweepstakes!
