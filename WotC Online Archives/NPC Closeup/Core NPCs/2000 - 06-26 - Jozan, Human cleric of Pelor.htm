NPC Closeup 06/26/2000


Jozan, Human cleric of Pelor


Determined to use the gifts granted him by his deity, Jozan has vowed to uphold the tenets of his faith. A skilled fighter with his mace, a capable archer with his crossbow, and a formidable spellcaster with an array of divine spells, Jozan is well equipped to answer Pelor's call to a life of adventure.

Below you'll get a glimpse of Jozan at three points in his adventuring career: at 1st, 4th, and 7th levels. (Remember that this isn't a full-blown character description, just a glimpse at how Jozan looks under 3rd Edition D&D rules.)
