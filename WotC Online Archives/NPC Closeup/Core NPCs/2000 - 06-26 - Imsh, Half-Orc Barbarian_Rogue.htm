Imsh, Half-Orc Barbarian/Rogue


There are those who would dismiss Imsh as a mindless savage, a fearsome brute capable only of destruction and havoc. But Imsh has learned much in the savage lands of his upbringing. He has learned to use his incredible strength to his advantage in battle. He has learned that cunning is a valuable tool for survival in the harshest of conditions. Most importantly, he has learned to channel the rage that burns within him, to control it until the heat of battle. Few opponents live to tell of their battles with Imsh. Fewer still walk away unscarred.
