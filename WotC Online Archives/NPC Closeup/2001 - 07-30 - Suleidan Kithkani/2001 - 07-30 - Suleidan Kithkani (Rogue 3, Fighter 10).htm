NPC Closeup 07/30/2001


Suleidan Kithkani
By Andy Collins and Greg Collins


Suleidan Kithkani at 13th Level

Suleidan Kithkani: Male human Rog3/Ftr10; CR 13; Medium-size humanoid; HD 3d6+3, 10d10+10; hp 78; Init +7; Spd 30 ft.; AC 20 (touch 14, flat-footed 20); Atk +16/ +11/ +6 melee (1d6+7/12-20/x2, +2 keen scimitar), +14/ +9 melee (1d6+2/19-20/x2, +1 short sword) or +16 ranged (1d2+3/x2, masterwork whip); SA sneak attack (+2d6); SQ evasion, uncanny dodge; AL CG (temporarily LE); SV Fort +10, Ref +10, Will +4; Str 16, Dex 16, Con 12, Int 10, Wis 8, Cha 13. Height 5 ft.10 in.

Skills and Feats: Balance +9, Bluff +7, Climb +10, Diplomacy +10, Gather Information +6, Hide +4, Intimidate +3, Jump +16, Move Silently +16, Profession (gambler) +3, Profession (sailor) +3, Sense Motive +4, Spot +2, Swim +5, Tumble +8, Use Rope +7; Ambidexterity, Dodge, Exotic Weapon Proficiency (whip), Improved Critical (scimitar), Improved Initiative , Improved Two-Weapon Fighting, Leadership, Mobility, Spring Attack, Two-Weapon Fighting, Weapon Focus (scimitar), Weapon Specialization (scimitar).

Evasion (Ex): If exposed to any effect that normally allows a character to attempt a Reflex saving throw for half damage, Suleidan suffers no damage with a successful saving throw.

Uncanny Dodge (Ex): Suleidan can react to danger before his senses would normally allow him to do so. He retains his Dexterity bonus to AC even when flat-footed or attacked by an invisible opponent.

Possessions:+2 chain shirt of silent moves, +2 keen scimitar, +1 short sword, masterwork whip, cloak of resistance +1, ring of protection +1, rope of climbing.
