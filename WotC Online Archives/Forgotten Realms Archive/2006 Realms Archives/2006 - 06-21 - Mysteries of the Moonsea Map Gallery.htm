Mysteries of the Moonsea Map Gallery


A book about a region wouldn't be complete without a few maps, and Mysteries of the Moonsea brings you a lot of them. Among other things, this Forgotten Realms supplement details major towns and adventure locations, as well as the important figures and groups that operate in and around the Moonsea.

Sincere thanks go to cartographers Kyle Hunter and Robert Lazzaretti.
