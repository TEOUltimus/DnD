Art Gallery 03/08/2006


Power of Faerûn Art Gallery


High-level characters exult! You can now challenge yourselves further with a wealth of information and useful campaign material in the Power of Faerûn campaign supplement. Get a glimpse of the power within this book by viewing the online art gallery.

Sincere thanks go to art director Mari Kolkowsky and artists Daarken, Eric Deschamps, Rick Drennan, Ron Lemen, Warren Mahy, William O'Connor, Lucio Parrillo, Francis Tsai, Franz Vohwinkel, and Kev Walker.
