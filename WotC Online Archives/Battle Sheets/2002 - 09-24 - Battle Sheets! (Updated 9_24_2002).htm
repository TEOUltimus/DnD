Battle Sheets!

We’ve just made your model collection twice as valuable! Now, besides using them in Chainmail skirmishes, put your models to work in your D&D games, too. This feature provides, for every figure, a Battle Sheet of D&D game statistics. No more time-consuming translations from one set of rules to another – just download a Battle Sheet, grab your model, and you’ve got an instant player character, monster, or NPC for your next game!

Statistics provided by Chainmail editor Jennifer Clarke Wilkes. Look for regular updates to this page as we add more Battle Sheets from each published set.

Note: Battle Sheets are created with an eye toward utility in combat. They do not include some statistics (found in standard monster or character sheets) that do not pertain to battle situations. Other elements, such as multiple creature combat tracking, have been added to facilitate using these creatures in combat.
