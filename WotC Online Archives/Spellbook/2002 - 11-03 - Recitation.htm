Spellbook
Recitation
By Rich Baker

Here is an improved prayer spell. The fact that it offers allies a luck bonus means the benefits of this spell stack with other bonuses characters may have active.

Recitation*
Conjuration (Creation)
Level: Clr 4
Components: V, S, DF
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 ft.
Area: All allies and foes within a 60-ft. radius burst centered on you
Duration: 1 round/level
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: Yes

By reciting a sacred passage or declaration from your holy writings, you bring special favor upon yourself and your allies while bringing disfavor to your foes. You and your allies gain a +2 luck bonus on attack rolls, weapon damage rolls, saves, and skill checks, while foes suffer a –2 penalty on such rolls.

* This is the fifth of six spells Rich originally wrote for Player's Option: Spells & Magic and has now updated for the new edition of the D&D game.
