Noble Houses of Waterdeep

The information presented in Noble Houses of Waterdeep, the new City of Splendors: Waterdeep web enhancement by Eric L. Boyd, provides complete information on Waterdeep's nobility. So if you've been looking for key information with which to set up intrigues in your Forgotten Realms campaign, look no further! Enhancement Preview

The blood of steel dragons runs through certain noble houses of Waterdeep. Below are the adjustments to the half-dragon template presented in the Monster Manual for half-steel dragons.

Half-Dragon, Steel

A half-steel dragon is the product of a union between a steel dragon and another creature. Because steel dragons prefer human form over draconic form, half-steel dragons are by far the most common kind of half-dragons in Faer√ªn. A typical half-steel dragon has dark gray hair, light gray scaled skin, and eyes of two different colors (one dark gray and one silver).

Half-dragons are fully detailed on page 146 in the Monster Manual. The adjustments for half-steel dragons are as follows.

Special Attacks: A half-steel dragon can breathe a 30-foot cone of corrosive (acid) gas.

Special Qualities: A half-steel dragon has a +10 racial bonus on all Fortitude saving throws against poison.

In the Realms

Half-steel dragons are most frequently encountered in large cities and metropolises, such as Neverwinter, Silverymoon, Suzail, and Waterdeep. In the City of Splendors, most half-steel dragons are members of the Confluence, a mysterious organization that battles the Unseen in Waterdeep's shadows.
